Aker was founded on three observations: 

1. The absence of top-tier multifamily operators outside of gateway cities.

2. A long-term trend of reshoring, and government investment in the innovation sector, including manufacturing, biotech, and education.

3. A systemic lifestyle shift due to hybrid work and the rise of cities that offer both urban conveniences and outdoor lifestyles.

Everything we do stems from a deep understanding of the communities we build for.

We’re inspired by large movements in industry and consumer behavior, including work-from-anywhere, a renewed passion for the outdoors, and a desire for human connection.
